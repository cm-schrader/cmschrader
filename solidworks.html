Solidworks

Parametric Modeling and Design

Solidworks is a parametric computer aided design program. I first began learning in when I took a CAD class my sophomore year of high school. Over the years, a majority of my projects have been done in solidworks. This includes my work with USLI, Busek, and others. My experience in parametric modeling helps me to create parts with clean feature trees that are easy to change later. My skill with Solidworks is also reasonably transferable to other parametric CAD software. My second most used CAD program is Fusion 360 and I have used other CAD packages like Onshape and Autodesk Inventor on occasion.
