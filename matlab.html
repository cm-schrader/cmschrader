MATLAB

Computational Mathematics and Scripting

MATALB is a programing language optimized for advanced mathematics. I first learned MATLAB when I descided to write a script to automate my Astronautics homework. Problems that took 30 minutes by hand were solved in just a few seconds. Since then, I've used it in a variety of applications, using a variety of features such as ODE solvers and the Aerospace and Controls toolboxes. Most of my work has been focused on dynamical and control simulation.
