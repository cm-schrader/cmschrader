Sigma Gamma Tau

Inducted 2019

Sigma Gamma Tau is a national aerospace engineering honors society that promotes academic excellence in the field. The WPI chapter runs a variety of programs for the betterment of the Aerospace Engineering Department and its students.

By committing myself to my studies and being an active member of the campus aerospace community, I was able to be inducted in 2019 as a sophomore. As part of SGT's tutoring program, I have tutored students in Astronautics and worked on a MATLAB workshop for students new to the program.
